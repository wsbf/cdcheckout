About

Features

  • Ability to check out albums to be reviewed
  • Ability to check out material (Vinyl/Cassette/CD) from the WSBF Library
  • Interfaces directly with WSBF database & accounts
  • Instant search of albums to be reviewed
  • Friendly user interface
  • Cross-platform
  • Has the WSBF Lady

About the Author

This program was written by David A. Cohen II, a senior studying computer science at Clemson University. He was formerly the computer engineer of Clemson's student-run radio station, WSBF-FM, and remains an active member, donating his time to the station for this and similar projects.
